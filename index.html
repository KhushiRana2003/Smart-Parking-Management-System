What do we Offer ?


✔  Single storey to multi-storey, fully automatic to semi automatic


With e-ticketing feature, manned managed parking, ANPR, boom barrier, partially automated as well as fully automated parking system, we are having an appropriate solution for you.


✔  Fully Automatic Parking System


Fully automatic parking system somehow looks like robotic valet parking. It refers to the man-less parking system where payment, entry and exit systems are automated by the means of state of the art technology.


✔  Contactless Parking System


RFID based parking management system is ideal for restricted areas where only authorized persons are allowed to access. The system is installed with an RFID reader and RFID tag integrated with boom barrier gate.


✔  Fastag Based Parking Solution


A FASTag is an RFID-based fare collection system. The RFID-enabled tags are rechargeable stickers affixed on the windshield of the vehicles. The FASTag stickers ensure less waiting time when crossing toll booths, thereby saving time as well as fuel.




Why Parking Management System is Important?


Technology has now made parking management system advanced and efficient. Earlier there used to be a lack of coordination, management and more. Till now deep human intervention is required by the parking management software to ensure accuracy and speed. It’s all because of the automatic parking management software that now with minimum human interaction vehicles can be parked in a specific parking area. Here are a few reasons why parking management systems are a favourable option:


✔  Space Optimization

Not able to utilize a parking area in an efficient manner is a big issue nowadays. The businesses also face big losses because of this. A parking management system allows the managers to have a 360-degree view of the parking lot. This is also the reason why they can easily direct the motorists and car drivers to the exactly available spaces.


✔   Cost Effective

The need for manpower also reduces with PMS and just two or three well-trained professionals can run the entire operation. Also, the system can detect the hardware in a jiffy and saves a lot of time.


✔   Easy to Use

Parking management software is very simple and effortless to use. It is very user-friendly and even if you have to train the staff, the cost will be minimum. Moreover very little or no maintenance is required; this resolves the issue of complications when the system can’t be used.


✔   Safe

Security and privacy are the two most important aspects of having a parking management system. Unlike the traditional way, the system embedded with the latest technology can keep a check on each and every vehicle entering and exiting the premises.
